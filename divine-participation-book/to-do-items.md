Concepts for emphasis
1. First is restatement with slightly different wording.
2. Second is transition emphasis. This happens at the end of sections or chapters. A short sentence or two summarizes what the reader must carry forward.
3. Third is explicit attention language. Occasionally it is helpful to signal to the reader that a point is important.
  - Words like: 
  -- notice
  -- this matters
  -- this reveals something important
