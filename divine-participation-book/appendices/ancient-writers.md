# Ancient Writers: Extended Context

This appendix provides extended excerpts from ancient Christian writers (primarily Ante-Nicene Fathers) to give readers fuller context for the citations used throughout the book. These substantial passages demonstrate that the book engages with these sources fairly and comprehensively, rather than proof-texting isolated phrases.

## How to Use This Appendix

### When Adding Excerpts:

1. **Provide substantial context** — Include enough surrounding text to show the author's full argument or thought
2. **Note the source** — Include work title, book/chapter/section numbers, and translation used
3. **Link to chapters** — Always note which chapter(s) cite this passage
4. **Include relevant context** — Brief biographical or historical notes help readers understand the author's situation

### Format for Each Entry:

```markdown
### Author Name (dates)

**Cited in:** [Chapter X — Title](../part-XX/chapter-XX.md)

**Context:** Brief biographical/historical context (2-3 sentences)

**Work:** *Title of Work*, Book X, Chapter Y

**Translation:** *(e.g., "Ante-Nicene Fathers, vol. X, p. Y" or "Schaff, vol. X" or specific translator)*

**Extended Excerpt:**

[Substantial passage here - multiple paragraphs to show full context]

**Notes:** *(Any relevant notes about the passage, its interpretation, or its significance)*
```

## Notes on Sources and Copyright

- **Public Domain:** Most ancient Christian writings are in the public domain, especially translations from the 19th and early 20th centuries (e.g., Ante-Nicene Fathers series, Nicene and Post-Nicene Fathers series)
- **Modern Translations:** Some modern translations may be copyrighted. When using these, shorter fair-use excerpts may be appropriate, with full citations
- **Primary Sources:** When possible, cite from established public domain collections (e.g., Schaff's *Ante-Nicene Fathers*, Migne's *Patrologia*)
- **Accessibility:** Consider providing links to online sources (e.g., CCEL.org, New Advent) where readers can access full texts
